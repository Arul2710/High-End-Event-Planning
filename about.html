Our Journey

From a dream to India's most loved event studio

LuxeEvents began in a small Bangalore studio with two passionate designers and a single floral arrangement. Today, we've grown into a full-service event planning powerhouse — but we've never lost that personal touch.

2012 – The Beginning

Founded in Indiranagar, Bangalore with a passion for beautiful celebrations and bespoke styling.

2015 – First 100 Weddings

Reached a milestone of 100 weddings, earning our first industry award for Best Wedding Styling Studio.

2018 – Corporate Expansion

Launched our corporate events division, styling galas for Fortune 500 brands across India.

2021 – Multi-City Presence

Expanded to Mumbai, Delhi, and Chennai — bringing LuxeEvents magic to more cities across India.

2024 – 500+ Events & Counting

Celebrated 500 events, 2,500+ happy clients, and recognition as India's #1 Event Planning Studio.

What Drives Us

Our Core Values

The principles that shape every event we touch and every relationship we build

Passion for Perfection

Every petal, candle, and chair placement matters. We obsess over details so you can be fully present in your celebration.

Artistry & Creativity

We don't do cookie-cutter events. Every concept is crafted from scratch, reflecting your unique personality and story.

Trust & Transparency

Honest pricing, clear communication, and no surprises — we build relationships that last well beyond the event day.

Excellence Always

From first consultation to final curtain call, we hold every element to the highest standard without exception.

Sustainable Styling

Beautiful events need not cost the earth. We source locally, repurpose florals, and minimise waste wherever possible.

People-First Culture

Our clients are family. We listen deeply, adapt quickly, and go the extra mile — because your joy is our greatest reward.
